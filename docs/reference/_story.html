Level of care

Choosing a disposition

Match acuity to the least-restrictive setting that's still safe. LOCUS (adult) and CALOCUS-CASII (youth) operationalize risk + function into a level; ASAM covers primary substance use.

LevelSettingTypical trigger
1OutpatientStable, low risk, good support
2IOP / PHPNeeds structure, no acute danger
3–4Residential / inpatientAcute risk or grave disability

Disposition pearls

Outpatient referral pathway is outpatient · IOP · PHP. A residential bed is not arranged from an outpatient clinic.

Arrange disposition fully before discharge — a verified, accepting resource beats a longer list of stale ones.
